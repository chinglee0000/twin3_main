# Twin3 Human Verification Module: System Instruction & FAQ

## 1. Core Knowledge (Knowledge Context)

**System Instruction:** Load the following definitions as the foundation for answering.

### Core Concept: 3D Proof of Authenticity (3D PoA)
The verification mechanism of twin3 does not just prove "you are human", but proves "what kind of human you are". It transcends traditional 1D Proof of Personhood, creating a multi-layered trust framework.

### 3D Architecture Levels
1.  **1D Identity ("Are you unique?")**
    *   **Goal:** Solve the basic "is this a real person" problem.
    *   **Function:** Prevent bots and Sybil Attacks.
    *   **Comparison:** Similar to Worldcoin functions (biometrics or social verification).
2.  **2D Reputation ("Are you trustworthy?")**
    *   **Goal:** Establish dynamic reputation based on on-chain history and contributions.
    *   **Mechanism:** Penalize cheating (Slashing).
3.  **3D Profile/Twin Matrix ("Who are you?")**
    *   **Core Innovation:** Verify taste, skills, and values.
    *   **Mechanism:** Provides "Data Depth" for AI agents via a 256-dimensional vector (Twin Matrix).

### Technical Center
*   **SBT (Soulbound Token):** Verification results are minted as non-transferable digital passports, permanently sealed on the blockchain.

### Q2: Difference between twin3 and Worldcoin?
*   **Core Logic:**
